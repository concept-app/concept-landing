Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2, 2026

Overview

Rework is a desktop application that runs locally on your computer. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how the application handles your data.

Data Collection

Rework does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data generated through your use of the application remains on your local device.

Local Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Conversation history and session data (stored in a local SQLite database)
  • API keys for AI providers (stored securely in your system keychain)
  • Application settings and preferences
  • OAuth tokens for connected services

Third-Party Services

When you use Rework with AI providers (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google), your prompts and conversations are sent directly to those providers using your own API keys. Please review the privacy policies of these providers for information on how they handle your data.

Data Security

API keys are stored using your operating system's secure keychain. Conversation data is stored in a local database file that you control and can delete at any time.

Your Control

Since all data is stored locally, you have full control over your information. You can delete your conversation history, remove API keys, or uninstall the application at any time to remove all associated data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact
